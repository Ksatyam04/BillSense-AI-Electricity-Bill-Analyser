# ⚡ BillSense — AI Electricity Bill Analyser

BillSense is a web app that helps Indian households understand, track, 
and reduce their electricity bills using AI.

## 📸 What It Does

- 📄 **Upload your electricity bill** (PDF) — auto-extracts units, amount, DISCOM
- 📊 **Dashboard** — see full bill breakdown with charts
- 🔮 **AI Forecast** — predicts your next 3 months of usage and cost
- 🔍 **Energy Audit** — finds your biggest energy guzzlers
- 💡 **Slab Optimizer** — shows exactly how many units to save to drop a slab
- 🌍 **Tariff Compare** — compares your bill across all 10 Indian DISCOMs
- 🤖 **AI Chat** — ask anything about your electricity bill
- 🌿 **Carbon Footprint** — calculates your CO₂ impact
- 📈 **Neighbourhood Benchmark** — see how you compare to your city average
- ❓ **What-If Planner** — "what if I add an EV or AC?"

## 🏙️ Supported DISCOMs (10 utilities)

BESCOM · MSEDCL · TNEB · BSES Rajdhani · BSES Yamuna · 
TSSPDCL · APSPDCL · CESC · UGVCL · JVVNL . TPDDL

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Backend** — Python, Flask, SQLite
- **Frontend** — HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript
- **AI** — Google Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Charts** — Chart.js
- **PDF Parsing** — pdfplumber
